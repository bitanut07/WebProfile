I am currently pursuing an Early Childhood Education Diploma at Humber Polytechnic. Through my studies, I have built a strong foundation in child development, play-based learning, and inclusive education. My experiences in classroom observations, lesson planning, and interactive activities have allowed me to support children's growth in meaningful ways. I have also developed skills in responsive caregiving and positive guidance to create safe and engaging learning environments. I am particularly passionate about integrating arts-based learning and creative expression into early education, using storytelling, music, and interactive play to enhance children's development. My goal is to make a lasting, positive impact by fostering a love for learning in a safe, engaging, and nurturing space.
